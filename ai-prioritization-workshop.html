Survey Design

9 leadership team members scored each of the 42 initiatives across 5 priority levels. Each vote was translated into a weighted score to capture urgency and consensus simultaneously.

ASAP = 4 pts — do in days/weeks
Next 6 months = 3 pts
6–12 months = 2 pts
2027+ = 1 pt
Wouldn't do = 0 pts

Column Thresholds

Each initiative's average weighted score (max 4.0) determines its column. Initiatives with 3+ "Wouldn't do" votes are automatically sent to Kill, regardless of score.

P1 Do Now Score ≥ 3.0 · Strong urgency + consensus
P2 Plan Next Score 2.5–2.99 · Valued but not urgent
Discuss Score < 2.5 · Split opinion — needs debate
Kill 3+ "Wouldn't do" votes · Remove from roadmap

Drag & Drop

This board is fully interactive. Drag any card to a different column to override the data-driven classification. Changes are tracked in real time — moved cards show a purple "moved" badge. Use the ↺ Reset button in the header to restore the original classification.

Cards marked Discuss are the most important for your workshop: they represent split opinions that need explicit leadership alignment before entering the roadmap.
